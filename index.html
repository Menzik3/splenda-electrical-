About Us

Splenda Electrical is a professional electrical contracting company operating within South Africa, and based within the Harry Gwala District. Founded in 2010, the company has successfully completed over 50 electrical infrastructure and domestic projects.

Our commitment is to deliver reliable, safe and high-quality electrical solutions for municipalities, businesses and households.
