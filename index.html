Splenda Electrical is a 100% black-owned professional electrical contracting & Consulting company based in the Harry Gwala District, South Africa. Founded in 2010, we have grown into a trusted name in electrical infrastructure, delivering quality and compliance across South Africa.

Our team of professional engineers, project managers and certified technicians bring decades of combined experience to every engagement — from large-scale municipal electrification to precision domestic installations and Consulting.
